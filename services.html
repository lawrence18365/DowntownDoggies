Luxury Dog Walking

Our premium dog walking service goes beyond a simple stroll. We offer:

  • Customized routes tailored to your dog's energy level and preferences
  • Small group walks (maximum 3 dogs) or private one-on-one sessions
  • Real-time GPS tracking and detailed post-walk reports
  • Hydration stations and gourmet treats during walks
  • Trained professionals skilled in canine body language and first aid
Book a Walk

Elite Pet Sitting

Our premium pet sitting service ensures your furry friend feels at home even when you're away. We provide:

  • Personalized care plans tailored to your pet's needs and routine
  • Daily updates with photos and detailed reports on your pet's activities
  • Medication administration and special dietary needs accommodation
  • Home security checks and plant watering services
  • 24/7 emergency support from our team of certified pet care professionals
Book a Sitter

Advanced Training

Our expert trainers use positive reinforcement techniques to help your dog reach their full potential. Our training programs include:

  • Customized training plans based on your dog's breed, age, and temperament
  • One-on-one sessions focusing on specific behavior issues or advanced skills
  • Group classes for socialization and real-world application of learned skills
  • Puppy training programs to start your new family member off on the right paw
  • Ongoing support and resources to reinforce training at home

Spa & Grooming

Treat your dog to a luxurious grooming experience with our premium spa services. We offer:

  • Full-service baths with premium, all-natural shampoos and conditioners
  • Expert haircuts and styling for all breed types
  • Relaxing massage treatments to reduce stress and promote wellbeing
  • Nail trimming and paw pad care for comfortable walks
  • Teeth cleaning and ear care for optimal health
