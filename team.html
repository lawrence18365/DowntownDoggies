Get to Know Us

Our Story

Downtown Doggies was born from a shared love for dogs and a vision to create a service that truly understands and caters to the unique needs of each furry friend. Founded in 2015 by lifelong dog enthusiasts, our journey began with a simple goal: to provide the kind of care we'd want for our own pets.

Today, we're proud to be a trusted part of the Inner West Sydney community, known for our personalized approach and unwavering commitment to canine happiness. Our team of certified professionals brings together years of experience, ongoing education, and a genuine passion for dogs of all shapes and sizes.

What sets us apart is our dedication to continuous learning and improvement. We regularly attend workshops, seminars, and training sessions to stay up-to-date with the latest in dog care, behavior, and training techniques. This commitment ensures that we're always providing the best possible care for your beloved pets.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We treat every dog with the love and care they deserve, understanding their unique needs and personalities.

Safety

The well-being of your pets is our top priority. We maintain the highest standards of safety in all our services.

Education

We're committed to ongoing learning and sharing our knowledge with pet owners to promote better care practices.

Trust

We build lasting relationships with our clients and their pets based on transparency, reliability, and consistent quality care.
